# Sunset Frequencies — Electronic Soundscapes at Golden Hour

"Sunset Frequencies" is an album built to be listened to as the sun dips below the horizon. The production favors warm analog synths and gentle breakbeats, creating an atmosphere that is both nostalgic and forward-looking.

Highlights:
- Lush synth textures
- Thoughtful pacing across the record
- A closing track that feels like a sigh
